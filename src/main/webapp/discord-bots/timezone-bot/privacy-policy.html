Timezone Bot - Privacy Policy

When you grab a timezone role (/timezone [tz_name] command), the bot saves the following information about you:

  • The ID of the server you grabbed a timezone role on
  • Your user ID
  • Your timezone
  • Your nickname and username - This is necessary for the /list-timezones command

This information is kept as long as you still have your timezone role, to keep it up-to-date. It is deleted:

  • immediately if you get rid of your timezone role with /remove-timezone
  • within 15 minutes if Timezone Bot is kicked from the server you had a timezone role in
  • within a few days if you leave the server you had a timezone role in

Some information is also logged in order to help with troubleshooting and checking the bot works as intended:

  • server joins/leaves
  • list of roles that have access to /toggle-times when the permissions of this command is updated on the server
  • Timezone Bot commands that are run
  • role changes done by the bot

Those logs are deleted after 7 days.

The collected information is only used for troubleshooting issues and to make the commands work. They are only accessible to the bot owner, and they are not used for anything else.

If you do not use the bot's slash commands, no information is logged or saved about you (and due to how Discord gateway intents work, said information is not even sent to the bot: it only asks for GUILDS events).
